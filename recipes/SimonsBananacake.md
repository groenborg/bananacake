## Ingredients
### Dry Ingredients
- 170g sugar
- 150g flour
- 2ts vanilla sugar 
- 1ts baking powder

### Wet Ingredients
- 3 eggs
- 3 bananas
- 100g margerine

## Method
1. Melt margerine
2. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl
3. Add Eggs and melted margerine and stur
4. Mosh the bananas
5. Mix in the bananas
6. Bake in oven at 200deg hot air. 

## Score
- Moistness: 9/10
- Sweetness: 9/10
- Overall: 8/10

## Comments
Supreme. For an optional you can mix in  2/3 of a pack of oreos.
